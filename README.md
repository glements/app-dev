# My Favorite Series: Stranger Things

*Genre*: Science Fiction, Horror, Mystery

- The 80s setting gives it a cool nostalgic vibe.

- The characters feel real and grow a lot throughout the seasons.
- The soundtrack and visuals are top-tier!
